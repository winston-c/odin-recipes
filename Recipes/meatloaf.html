Classic Meatloaf


Renee Comet (photographer), Public domain, via Wikimedia Commons

Description

Believe it or not, despite the name, the secret to the best meatloaf is fresh, finely diced vegetables.
Perfect for fall and winter evenings, this meatloaf is best enjoyed with a side of gravy and mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

Meatloaf Ingredients

  • 1 carrot, coarsely chopped
  • 1 rib celery, coarsely chopped
  • 1/2 onion, coarsely chopped
  • 1/2 bell pepper, coarsely chopped
  • 4 white mushrooms, coarsely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, coarsely chopped
  • 2.5 lbs ground chuck
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian herbs
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup plain breadcrumbs
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil

Glaze Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons dijon mustard
  • hot pepper sauce to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven to 325 degrees
  2. In a food processor, place the onion, red bell pepper, mushrooms, carrot, celery, and garlic
  3. Pulse until vegetables are finely chopped, almost to a puree.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, place the vegetables, ground chuck, Worcestershire sauce, and egg.
  5. Season with Italian herbs, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper.
  6. With a wooden spoon, mix gently.
  7. Now with your hands, incorporate the breadcrumbs and mix for approximately 1 minute.
