Good 'Ol Fashioned Pancakes


Janine from Mililani, Hawaii, United States, CC BY 2.0, via Wikimedia Commons

Description

This is a recipe for the classic, delicious pancakes like the ones you know and love from your childhood.

This easy recipe is sure to satisfy. Top with your favorite maple syrup and a pat of butter.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3.5 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1.25 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions

  1. Sift together the baking powder, flour, sugar, and Salt in a large bowl.
  2. In a seperate bowl, mix together your milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. With your hand or a spoon, hollow out the center pour in your egg mixture.
  4. Lightly oil a frying pan or griddle over medium-to-high heat.
  5. Deposit approximately 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake.
  6. Brown on both sides. Serve hot.
